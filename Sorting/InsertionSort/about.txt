Insertion Sort is a simple sorting algorithm.

It works like:

“Jaise tum cards ko haath mein sort karte ho —
ek-ek card uthao aur sahi jagah pe daal do.”

How it works (Idea)

Second element se start hota hai

Us element ko left side ke sorted part mein
sahi position pe insert karta hai

Ye process har element ke liye repeat hota hai
